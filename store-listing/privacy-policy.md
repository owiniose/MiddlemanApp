# Privacy Policy — Middleman

**Last updated: March 30, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how Middleman ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Middleman mobile application (the "App").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Information you provide to us:**
- Name, email address, and password when you create an account
- Phone number and delivery addresses
- Order details including items, delivery address, and payment method
- Reviews and ratings you submit for vendors

**Information collected automatically:**
- Device push notification token (to send order updates)
- Order history and activity within the app

**Information from third-party services:**
- Payment information is processed by Flutterwave and is not stored on our servers
- Profile and menu images are stored via Cloudinary

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Process and deliver your orders
- Send you real-time order status notifications
- Provide customer support
- Improve the app and our services
- Display your reviews to other users

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only as necessary to operate the service:

- **Vendors** receive your name, phone number, delivery address, and order details to fulfil your order
- **Flutterwave** processes card and bank transfer payments securely
- **Cloudinary** stores images you upload (profile photos, vendor images)
- **Google Firebase** powers our database, authentication, and notifications
- **Google Gemini** powers the Mido AI assistant — your messages to Mido may be processed by Google's AI services

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

---

## 5. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications to update you on your order status (e.g. order accepted, on the way, delivered). You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You may at any time:
- Update your profile information within the app
- Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below
- Opt out of push notifications via your device settings

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Middleman is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
