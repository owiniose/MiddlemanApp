## Full Description (4000 characters max — Play Store)

Middleman is Uyo's all-in-one delivery app. Order food, groceries, medicines, and send packages — all from one place, delivered straight to your door.

**Order from your favourite spots**
Browse hundreds of restaurants, shops, pharmacies, and courier services across Uyo. From a late-night meal to everyday groceries, Middleman gets it to you fast.

**Track every order in real time**
Know exactly what's happening with your order — from the moment the vendor accepts it, to when it's on its way, to when it arrives at your door.

**Meet Mido, your AI shopping assistant**
Not sure what to order? Mido is Middleman's built-in AI assistant. Ask for recommendations, find the best deals, or get help choosing from a vendor's menu — Mido has you covered.

**Pay your way**
Choose how you want to pay — cash on delivery, bank transfer, or card. Secure card payments are powered by Flutterwave.

**Customise your order**
Add special instructions, choose sizes, pick toppings, and customise every item exactly the way you like it.

**Save your addresses**
Save your home, office, or any delivery address for faster checkout every time.

**Built for Uyo**
Middleman is designed specifically for Uyo, Akwa Ibom — with local vendors, local delivery zones, and local pricing.
